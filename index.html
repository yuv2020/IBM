What is E-sports?

Esports, short for electronic sports, refers to organized competitive video gaming. It involves professional players or teams competing against each other in multiplayer video games across various genres such as first-person shooters, real-time strategy games, fighting games, and more. Esports events can range from small-scale local tournaments to large international championships, attracting massive audiences both online and in-person. Just like traditional sports, esports often feature skilled players, dedicated fan bases, sponsorship deals, and prize pools, making it a rapidly growing and highly lucrative industry.

What is Metaverse?

The concept of the Metaverse refers to a virtual universe or collective virtual shared space where users can interact with a computer-generated environment and other users in real-time. It is often depicted as a fully immersive and interconnected digital realm that combines elements of augmented reality (AR), virtual reality (VR), and the internet.
